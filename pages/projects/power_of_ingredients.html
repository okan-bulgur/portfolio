Power of Ingredients

Discover the Power of Ingredients in Your Kitchen

Power of Ingredients is a smart and social recipe management app designed to help you make the most of what you already have at home. Effortlessly scan your shopping receipts, track your stock of ingredients and discover recipes that fit your pantry.

But it's more than a cooking assistant. It's also a community. Follow other users, discover their favorite recipes and get inspired by trends in your community. You can also create your own recipes and share them with your followers.

From planning meals and managing shopping lists to organizing recipe portions and collaborating with friends, Power of Ingredients brings convenience, creativity and socialization to your kitchen.

Your Smart Kitchen Hub.


From discovering favorite recipes to managing your storage, shopping lists, cooking history and receipt history, the home screen gives you quick access to everything that matters.

Tired of Wondering What to Cook?

The app suggests delicious recipes using your own ingredients. You can also explore all recipes or jump to your favorites with just a tap.

All Set to Start Cooking!

Check the ingredients, follow the steps, and enjoy a homemade meal that suits your taste.

Turn Your Receipt Into Recipes

Snap or upload your shopping receipt and let the magic begin! Instantly capture every ingredient without typing a single word. Shopping just got smarter.

Save Ingredients in One Tap

Review what the app found and with one simple tap, send all your fresh ingredients straight into your storage. Fast, easy, and ready for your next recipe.

Share Your Signature Recipes

Create your own recipes from ingredients to step-by-step instructions and let others get inspired by your kitchen creations. Whether it’s a family classic or your own invention, your recipe deserves the spotlight!

Your Culinary Identity

Track your recipes, see who follows your creations, and explore what others are cooking. It’s not just about food, it’s about connecting through taste.

Your Digital Pantry

Keep track of everything in your kitchen, all in one place. Add, remove, or update your ingredients effortlessly and always know what you have before you cook or shop.

Plan Now, Cook Later

Save your favorite recipes for later and cook when the time is right! If you have all the ingredients, great. Your storage gets updated instantly. If you're missing anything, no worries. We’ll help you to create a shopping list.

Missing Something? We’ve Got You.

Name your list and we will automatically add the missing ingredients you need to make the recipe you have planned. No more guessing what to buy - just plan, shop and cook.

Your Smart Shopping List

We've listed what you need, now it's your turn to look, shop and cook. Your pantry will stay up to date as you move forward.
