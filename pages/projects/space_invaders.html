Resume

Space Invaders

Inspired by the legendary Space Invaders, this game brings a fresh and fun experience with more action, more strategy, and a lot more aliens!


You start with a single spaceship, and your goal is simple: destroy as many aliens as possible, collect gold, and chase the highest score. Gold drops randomly while you play — collect enough and you can unlock brand new ships. Each ship has its own personality: different health, speed, bullet styles, firing rates, and damage.



What makes it special?


  • Multiple alien types to keep things interesting
  • Unlockable ships with unique strengths
  • 4 exciting power-ups dropped randomly during the game:
    • +1 health
    • Temporary speed boost
    • Faster bullet rate
    • Stronger bullets
  • Power-up durations increase as you level up


On-screen info while playing:


  • Level progress bar (game ends at 100%)
  • Lives remaining
  • Username display
  • Current and highest score
  • Live High Score Table with global ranking
  • In-game notification when a personal high score is broken


Whether you're a fan of old-school arcade shooters or just looking for something fast and addictive, this game offers a great mix of retro charm and modern gameplay.
