Wallets of Wallets

Wallets of Wallets turns group spending into a smooth experience — no more awkward bill splits or lost receipts. Just one wallet, shared by many.


What does it do?


  • Create a wallet for any purpose — a trip with friends, a group gift, or even a donation drive.
  • Invite friends to join your wallet.
  • Everyone adds money using their bank cards.
  • Admins can then spend or withdraw from the shared balance.


Why use it?


Let’s say you're going on a trip with friends. Instead of tracking who paid for what or sending each other endless payment links, you all put your share into one wallet. One or more trusted friends act as admins and handle all the payments — easy, transparent, and stress-free.
